Project SD-516 "Stellar Degenerator"

IY-1446::45 => SD-516 "Stellar Degenerator" captured from Sivv civilization near the end of the War of Aggression.
IY-1447::23 => Collapse of Sivv civilization, evaluation of war climate determines no need of SD-516. Stored in hyperstellar demiplane for future use.
IY-1562::86 => SD-516 employed against Rykkal civilization.
IY-1563::30 => Extinction of the Rykkal.
IY-1563::45 => Destructive potential of SD-516 deemed too high for any use. Sealed in demiplane indefinitely.

SD-516 "Stellar Degenerator" is a Class 5 heavy armament capable of siphoning stellar energy from the target, resulting in loss of energy and imminent heat death of target system. Activation requires unanimous approval of all active governing bodies holding jurisdiction over military operations.

WARNING: All research on SD-516 has been halted indefinitely following recommendation from the Council of War.

SD-516 is to be stored only in specifically designated Hyperstellar Demiplane 516. Access requires Tier 1 authorization.

Hyperstellar Demiplane 516 is stabilized in the gravitational orbit of twelve main sequence stars. Control of the Gate is accessible through command stations located on designated planetoids orbiting the stars.
